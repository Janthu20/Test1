PROJECT DETAILS

Voice Coil Actuator Design

A research-based approach to designing high-precision actuators for medical ventilator flow control valves.

SolidWorks Magnetic Simulation Medical Engineering Research

Project Overview

The objective of this project is to design a Voice Coil Actuator (VCA) capable of precise linear motion control for a ventilator valve. Unlike solenoid valves, VCAs offer proportional control, which is critical for regulating airflow in medical devices.

Technical Implementation

The design process involves calculating the required Lorentz force based on the valve's pressure requirements. I am currently using FEMM (Finite Element Method Magnetics) to simulate the magnetic flux density and optimize the coil winding geometry for maximum efficiency.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Maintaining a consistent magnetic field throughout the stroke length.
Solution: Designing a custom iron core geometry to focus the magnetic flux lines directly through the coil path.
